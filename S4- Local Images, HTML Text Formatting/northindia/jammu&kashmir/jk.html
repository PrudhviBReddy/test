Famous Food in Jammu & Kashmir

Rajma Chawal

What is it: Red kidney beans or rajma curry served with rice. The sweetness of kidney beans are exclusive to Jammu, hence the dish is quite a popular one. What does it taste like: Spices like chili and turmeric powder along with onions and tomatoes make this main dish a delectable one. The mild aroma that it imparts is a result of using fresh coriander leaves.

Patisa

What is it: Alternatively known as “son papri” or “sohan papdi”, these little cubes are prepared from a mixture of gram and maida flour together. What does it taste like: The soft yet smooth texture satisfies your sweet teeth as milk and syrup do their part.
